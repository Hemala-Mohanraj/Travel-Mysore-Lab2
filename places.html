Famous Tourist Places

Mysore Palace

The royal palace of Mysore is known for its grand architecture, history, and beautiful illumination during festivals.

Chamundi Hills

A spiritual and scenic hill offering panoramic views of Mysore city and the famous Chamundeshwari Temple.

Brindavan Gardens

Famous for musical fountains, lighting shows, and beautifully maintained gardens near KRS dam.

Mysore Zoo

One of the oldest zoos in India with a wide variety of animals and a great place for families and children.

St. Philomena’s Cathedral

A stunning Gothic-style church inspired by European architecture, one of the tallest churches in Asia.

Karanji Lake

A peaceful nature spot with boating, bird watching, and walking trails surrounded by greenery.
